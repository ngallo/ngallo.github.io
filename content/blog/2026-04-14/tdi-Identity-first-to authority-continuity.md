On March 3, 2026, LF Decentralized Trust hosted the session *“From Identity-First to Authority Continuity”*, presented by Nicola Gallo.

<!--more-->

If you are interested in authority propagation across distributed systems, trust boundaries, and AI agents, you can watch the session and access the presentation materials here.

**Presentation summary.** This talk argues that the real unsolved problem is not identification, but authority propagation through execution. AI agents do not introduce a new class of security problems; they make existing structural failures impossible to ignore. PIC reframes the model from authority-as-possession to authority-as-continuity: a subject creates authority by expressing intent, and after that authority must only be continued, never re-created. Under this view, every hop in a distributed execution must prove causal continuity, preserve the immutable origin, and respect monotonically non-expanding authority.
